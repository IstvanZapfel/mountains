About us

We are Two Travelers looking for adventures whose hearts forever belong to the gorgeous Carpathian Mountains which became our guide and shelter and the best teacher ever. Our love began from the first forest path and a mushrooms smell, from the slashing hailstorm on the steep uphill climb, from the weighty backpacks and slippery wet rocks, from the unforgettable views and sunsets which worth all inconveniences that city dwellers might face in wild nature.
